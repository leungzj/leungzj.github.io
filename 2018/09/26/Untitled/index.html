navicat连接mysql提示"not allowed to connect"

2018-09-26

1、命令行登进mysql

GRANT ALL PRIVILEGES ON . TO ‘username‘@’%’ IDENTIFIED BY ‘password’ WITH GRANT OPTION;
commit;
flush privileges;

2、如果还是无法连接，查看iptables，要放开3306端口
iptables -A INPUT -p tcp –dport 3306 -j ACCEPT
